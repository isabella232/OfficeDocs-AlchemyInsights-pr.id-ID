[Menonaktifkan OneDrive situs untuk beberapa pengguna](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/manage-user-profiles#disable-onedrive-creation-for-some-users)

[Mengontrol akses ke SharePoint Data OneDrive online dan data berdasarkan lokasi jaringan](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/control-access-based-on-network-location)

[Menonaktifkan pengaturan OneDrive Sync](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#DisablePersonalSync)
